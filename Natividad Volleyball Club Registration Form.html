Data Privacy and Consent Form

Natividad Volleyball Club Blazing Phoenix is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with applicable data protection laws. This Data Privacy and Consent Form outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

1. Collection of Personal Information

We collect personal information such as your full name, date of birth, gender, address, email address, phone number, emergency contact details, and medical conditions to facilitate your registration and participation in the club.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

Your personal information will be used for the following purposes:

  • Processing your registration and membership.
  • Contacting you regarding club activities, events, and updates.
  • Ensuring your safety by maintaining emergency contact and medical information.
  • Improving our services and programs.

3. Data Sharing

Your personal information will not be shared with third parties except:

  • With your explicit consent.
  • When required by law or to protect the safety of club members.
  • With coaches, medical personnel, or event organizers, as necessary for club activities.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Withdraw your consent and request deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations.

6. Consent

By signing up for the Natividad Volleyball Club Blazing Phoenix, you consent to the collection, use, and processing of your personal information as described in this form. You may withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us at privacy@natividadvolleyballclub.org.

For any questions or concerns regarding your personal information, please contact us at info@natividadvolleyballclub.org.
